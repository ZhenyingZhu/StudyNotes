# Note Taking

## Mind Map

- Central Idea: Every map begins with a single concept, often represented by an image or bold keyword in the center.
- Radiant Branches: Main themes radiate from the center like branches of a tree.
- Keywords: Professional techniques suggest using only one keyword per branch to keep the map open to new associations.
- Visual Cues: Using at least three colors and various images or icons helps stimulate memory and differentiate themes.
- Associations: Curved lines connect related ideas, showing the flow from broad topics to specific "twigs" or sub-details.
